Hey, I'm Bilyana. I'm based in Ireland and while I'm hiding from the temperamental weather here (yes, even in the summer), I code things for the internet!

Hey, I'm Bilyana - a digital craftswoman, born in Bulgaria, based in Ireland. I've been a big fan of computers and video games (also tattoos, doggos, gym, food, art, and food) ever since I was a lil' human.

Finally, in 2020, I decided to teach myself how to build stuff for the web, and discovered my love for it. A bit weird, right? It's a space of constant improvement and new technology, so it keeps me busy and interested.

I use HTML, CSS, Tailwind CSS, JavaScript and WordPress. Though constantly changing, my focus now is on JavaScript and Three.js and I'm always up for learning new things.

I also like writing. Nothing in particular, mainly whatever is in my head on the day. If you want to check out my words you can do so here.
